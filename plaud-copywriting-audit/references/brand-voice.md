## Voice = constant. Tone = adaptive.

Plaud has one voice — calm, refined, quietly confident. Tone adjusts by context, channel, and audience.

**We sound like:** A trusted Chief of Staff — intelligent, proactive, composed.
**We do NOT sound like:** A tool trying to impress through buzzwords or exaggerated claims.

---

## The four voice principles

| Voice Pillar | Expressed As | In Practice |
|---|---|---|
| **Intelligent** | Clarity with Depth | Precision, simplicity, insight. Make complexity easy, not shallow. |
| **Professional** | Confidence without Noise | Calm authority, grounded reasoning. No hype. We guide, we don't shout. |
| **Human** | Human-Centered Intelligence | Write for collaboration, not replacement. Every sentence serves understanding and connection. |
| **Secure** | Trusted Integrity | Reinforce trust, transparency, and responsibility. Dependable, transparent, safe. |

Together: intelligent yet human, composed yet confident, innovative yet credible.

## Tone dimensions

Calibrate these 4 dimensions based on context:

| Dimension | Formal end | Conversational end |
|---|---|---|
| Formality | PR, enterprise, investor | Social, lifestyle, UX |
| Emotion | Calm & composed (product, technical) | Warm & empathetic (support, community) |
| Energy | Crisp & decisive (campaigns, launches) | Measured & reflective (thought leadership) |
| Confidence | Strong & assured (industry leadership) | Supportive & humble (empowering users) |

## Language system

**Verbs to use:** capture, extract, utilize, amplify, align, transform, act, empower, bridge
**Nouns to use:** intelligence, insight, context, decision, action, companion, presence, structure
**Adjectives (sparingly):** intelligent, seamless, reliable, trusted, refined

**Never use:** amazing, revolutionary, cutting-edge, next-level, game-changing, abstract metaphors, filler superlatives
